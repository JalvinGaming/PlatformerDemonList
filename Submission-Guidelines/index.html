Rules

  • The level must be a Platformer level.
  • The level must be verified legitimately, without any hacks disallowed by the Pointercrate Demonlist.
  • Levels must not require more than 15 clicks per second at any point during the level.

How to get your Level submitted

Write an E-Mail to hellofellowdogz@gmail.com including the Level ID, a thumbnail of the level, a video of the level's verification and a suggested spot on the leaderboard. After consideration from both me and the community, I will put it on the demonlist.
